ABOUT US

GOVLINK SOLUTIONS INC is a technology and management company based in the USA, with a solid capital base, sound management team as well as a dedicated team of professionals that are committed to building long-lasting business relationships.

Our Mission

Create client satisfaction
By being focused on our clients, as set out in our sector strategies

Drive professional excellence
By being effective, at our best, every time for every client, and for ourselves

Achieve commercial success
By being competitive, leveraging the best technology that aligns with our client's needs

Provide employee fulfillment
By treating everyone with dignity and respect recognizing that our employees are one of our key assets

Our Core Values

Progress

  • We actively develop ourselves as individuals and professionals

Respect

Excellence

Drive

  • We constantly stretch our thinking to find extra value for our clients and their end-users

  • We stay focused and persevere to achieve our goals

GOVLINKS’s universe of creative thinkers is governed by our core values and centered around our clients – you. We’re joined-up across sectors and geographies, giving you access to exceptional breadth and depth of expertise and experience. Over 80% of the work we do is for long-term or repeat clients, which we think underlines the great service we offer.
